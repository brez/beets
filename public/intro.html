Mo Peeps, Mo Problems

Introducing more people to an overall effort, introduces more problems. Lots of attempts to introduce a variety of processes have failed likely due to the dull / clinical nature of said processes. SFFP (Sucka Free Flow Process) attempts to make this more interesting by engaging peeps in a spoken word, hip hop session where they think far and deep about not only the requirement itself but how it's spit..

Process

  • Tag It - marker on the wall, sharpee on paper
  • Rhyme It - work out the flow with the Mixer
  • Battle It - get a consensus on meaning / flow
  • Reprazent It - collaborative spoken word session

Roles

  • Flow Manager: Manages the overall flow
  • Mix Master: Rocks the Mixer in support
  • Souljas: Contribute to the Conversation
  • Witness: someone remains impartial / keeps the peace
